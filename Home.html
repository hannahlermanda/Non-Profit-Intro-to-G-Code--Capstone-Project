What We Do

Give South Central children and their families a safe haven to grow 💞 🏠


"I really want to start a place for all the children to go to - to be safe, to feel safe."

-Debrah Constance, Founder


Since 1993, A Place Called Home has made it its mission to provide the canvas for South Central youth to mold their futures with purpose. APCH strives to provide resources and opportunities for at-risk young people and their families, so that they are empowered to take control of their lives. Since our founding and our original 12 members, we have grown and served over 20,000 youths and their families in the South Central area. We are determined to continue helping the future thrive through the investment in our children and their families.

Who We Serve

The youth of South Central Los Angeles, their families, and the community 💫


APCH fosters us even though we come from a tough neighborhood. Anything is possible with hard work, mentorship, and community.

-Cindy Diaz, APCH Alumni

A Place Called Home serves youth ages 8-24 and their family by providing resources that contribute to their holistic growth and development. Residents of the community we serve are historically under-resourced and face adverse challenges, such as high unemployment and crime rates and low graduation rates and income. ACPH gives developing youth a safe place to come to provide resources and a supportive community that encourages them to foster their dreams and talents to fruition for lifelong success and happiness.

Our Programs

"We give them a space to think, a space to create, and a space to challenge their own thoughts, and at the very least, tools to navigate the world."

- Dr. Lucretia Taylor, Program Director (Teen & Young Adult Services)

Our programs include free year-round services such as:

  • Education Services
  • Art, Music, Dance, and other Creative Expressions Program
  • Community Engagement and Outreach
  • Health and Nutrition
  • Bridge to the Future Programs
  • Rise at ACPH
